    What even is this??

    ex3 is an experimental utility Discord bot. It's got 70+ commands, spanning from AI image generation to moderation to dumb internet bullshit you didn't know you needed until now.

    Roadmap

    Planned future features include:

    • YouTube search + downloads (yes, for "educational" purposes)
    • Server management tools that don't suck
    • More image generation nonsense

    However, ex3 is built with zero data collection. It can't read messages. That's on purpose - these limitations keep the bot lean and respectful, even if they do make some features annoyingly impossible.

    Stuff that's never being added

    • Games. In theory, text-based games are cool, but heavily overshadowed by proper apps inside Discord, at least in my eyes.
    • Explicit NSFW commands
    • Voice channel music (YT Watch Together and abundance of other bots - why?). I recommend using Jockie Music instead.
    • Anything that needs your data - because I don't want it
