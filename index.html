ex3: a highly experimental bot with 70+ random commands

Some quick features

  • 70+ random commands, from basic moderation, to fun AI-powered commands, search tools, and doing all kinds of stuff with RESTful APIs
  • Plug and play: no setup required and works externally and in DMs
  • Collects zero data on you (click)

Some features, but more nerdy

  • AI image generation, text translation, text-to-speech, and Whisper transcription
  • Query Wikipedia, Hacker News, Spotify, Last.fm, GitHub, Mastodon, and YouTube
  • Judge your Last.fm music taste with AI in four different flavours
  • Perform WHOIS lookups, convert timezones, and calculate exchange rates
  • Generate memes, mock text, or play /rock-paper-scissors
  • Search Steam games or profiles, IP lookups, country data, BeamNG.drive vehicles
  • /mistranslate - puts text through Google Translate many times for an absurdly mistranslated result
  • No setup or configuration required!
